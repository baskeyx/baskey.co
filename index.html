Dan Baskeyfield / Creative Developer

I'm Dan Baskeyfield, a web developer based in London with a passion for art, design, tech, sport and fitness.

Since graduating with a degree in Internet Computing I've produced work for brands such as Aston Martin, Rolls-Royce, Topshop, Ivy Park and McLaren.

Over the last five years I've homed in on content delivery for fashion brands at Arcadia Group and Oasis. I've been responsible for leading projects such as the Ivy Park launch, Topshop's biannual Fashion week campaigns, and Christmas campaigns.

I'm also a huge advocate of the agile methodology and working in a scrum environment. Over the year I've worked at Oasis I've introduced sprints and stand ups and currently act as Scrum Master/Product Owner developing when I can.
